## 2.7 The "One Thing" Differentiator

### THE ONE THING:

**Designer-level construction (structured boning, double-lined chiffon, premium tulle) at an accessible price point‚Äîwithout the luxury markup.**

### Why it matters:

Most affordable formal dresses cut corners on construction, leading to slipping, transparency, and cheap-looking results. Most designer dresses with proper construction cost $2,000+. Auralo bridges this gap: you get the stay-put security, opacity, and luxe finish of a designer piece without the four-figure price tag.

### Proof it works:

- Internal grip lining and boning tested for all-night wear
- Double-layer construction = zero transparency
- Premium tulle that photographs beautifully (not stiff or costume-y)
- Flattering on multiple body types due to structured design
- Customers repeatedly ask "Is this designer?!"
