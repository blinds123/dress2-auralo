# RESEARCH-GEARED PROMPTING GUIDE

To ensure the 35 image prompts are of "World Class" quality, the AI must follow these rules when translating Phase 1 Research into Phase 2 Prompts:

### 1. Demographic Matching
*   **If the Dream Customer is "Luxury Lara" (High income, 30s):** The locations in prompts should be "Upscale penthouses", "Private jets", "Luxury spas". Lighting should be "Golden hour" or "High-key studio".
*   **If the Dream Customer is "Rave Roxy" (Gen Z, festivals):** Locations should be "Industrial warehouses", "Neon-lit festivals", "Street style". Lighting should be "Strobe", "Hard flash", or "Cyberpunk neon".

### 3. Native Aesthetic
*   The "TikTok-style" reply bubbles must look NATIVE.
